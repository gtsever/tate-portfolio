New Era Management System (NEMS)

Purdue University - Jan. 2024 - Apr. 2024.

The New Era Management System, known internally as "NEMS" was a group project assigned in CNIT280, Systems Analysis and Design.

My group was presented with a case study - a landlord who wants a new way to manage information regarding her properties, tenants, and payments. Through the use of SCRUM and its methods, our semester-long project was completed in April 2024. You can view the project here.

Note - the website is just a prototype. Credentials to access specific sections are available upon request!

Skills used: Diagramming, SCRUM, Systems Analysis, Web Design

Tools used: Visual Paradigm, Microsoft Sharepoint, Wix Website Builder, and a whole lot of brainpower.
